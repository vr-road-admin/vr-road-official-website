About Us

VR-Road is a transforming driver education through immersive virtual reality technology. Our mission is to make learning to drive safer, smarter, and more engaging—especially for the next generation of drivers.

  • Integrated data analytics platform for tracking learner progress and performance insights.
  • Immersive virtual reality experience enhanced with a physical motion simulator for realistic driving feedback.

Why Choose VR-Road for Driver Education?

VR-Road blends cutting-edge VR technology, real-world driving feedback, and performance analytics to offer a smarter, safer, and more engaging learning experience — especially for tech-savvy learners and those with road anxiety.

01 How is VR-Road different from traditional driver training?

Unlike static instruction or basic driving videos, VR-Road uses immersive virtual reality combined with a motion simulator to replicate real-life driving — all in a risk-free environment.

02 Who can benefit from using VR-Road?

High schools, driver ed programs, and training centers benefit from scalable, safe teaching tools. It's also ideal for learners with anxiety or those looking for more interactive and modern learning formats.

03 What insights can your data platform provide?

Our integrated analytics system tracks user behavior, reaction times, and progress over time—giving instructors actionable insights and helping learners see their growth clearly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how VR-Road works, who it's for, and how to get started with immersive driving education.

What’s included with the $25,000 VR-Road simulator?

Each unit includes a full-motion driving simulator, a single ultra-wide display, VR headset, custom-built training software, instructor dashboard, and lifetime software updates.

How does the revenue sharing model work?

In addition to the simulator purchase, institutions can open access to public users and share a percentage of the profits with VR-Road. The split is flexible and negotiated based on location and scale.

Who is VR-Road designed for?

Our solution is ideal for high schools, driver education programs, vocational training centers, and individuals with driving anxiety who benefit from a safe, controlled simulation environment.

What kind of training scenarios are included in the simulator?

VR-Road includes a variety of real-world driving scenarios such as city streets, highways, night driving, and adverse weather conditions. These are designed to prepare learners for challenges they’ll face on the road — all in a safe, controlled environment.

Does the system collect and track user data?

Absolutely. Our built-in data platform tracks each learner’s sessions, reaction times, decision-making, and improvement trends — perfect for instructors and performance reviews.
